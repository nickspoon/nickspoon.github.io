Proof Systems and Error-Correcting Codes Workshop
When: Friday, December 5, 2025, 10am-3:30pm
Where: Cornell Tech, NYC
What: A one-day workshop bringing together researchers working at the intersection of proof systems and error-correcting codes, both in academia and industry. This workshop is an opportunity to share new results, open questions, and perspectives on the future of the field. Anyone with an interest in either topic is welcome to attend!
Registration is free but mandatory. Please register by December 1.
